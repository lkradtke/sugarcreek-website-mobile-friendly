What to do around town

The Shops on Main Street

Come enjoy the many different shops on our main street. We have a fairy garden store, gift shops and stop in at Esther's Bakery for a fresh baked pie.

Giant Cuckoo Clock

It's world famous! Our cuckoo clock is the largest of its kind. Hang out and watch the clock come to life every half-hour when the little Swiss dancers and Alpine band make their appearances.

Alpine Hills Museum

The perfect place to kickoff your Amish Country adventure. Learn about Amish history and culture and the impact of the Swiss immigrants on this areas.

Broad Run Cheesehouse

No better way to cap off a day in Amish Country than sampling the cheese and wine! Broad Run produces award-winning cheese and wine that are also available for purchase.
